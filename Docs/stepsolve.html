Cafe Sudoku

Step Solving
Cafe Sudoku employs a method of display how it works
called "Step Solve."

Step solve works by showing you exactly what Cafe
Sudoku is doing to solve the puzzle. It shows you
what puzzle cell is being examined, and also shows
how that cell is being changed.

While in the middle of solve step, you have the
options of taking the next step towards solving,
letting Cafe Sudoku solve the rest of the puzzle,
or stopping, and solving the rest yourself.
